> [!TIP]
> The all-new Practices Platform is finally here! We've built a full-fledged offline code-learning experience from the ground up. Stop context switching out to external editors—solve top-tier coding constraint problems right where you map your progress.

### What's New?

#### 🎓 Integrated LeetCode IDE

- A completely transparent **Split-Pane Workspace** integrated directly into your offline browser session.
- **Left Pane:** Displays the 100% original, math-rendered problem descriptions scraped real-time directly from LeetCode. No markdown cutdowns, no missing constraints.
- **Right Pane:** A full instance of `Monaco Editor` (the core of VS Code) that highlights JavaScript syntax, providing you with a comfortable, professional canvas to draft your problem-solving approaches.

#### 🗂 Real Interview Problem Sets

- A library of top questions sorted by real frequency from over **500 tech giants** like Amazon, Google, and Meta.
- Search instantly across companies and individual constraints with our Fuse.js client-side engine.
- Toggle question progress status (To-do, Solving, Solved, Review) with one click from the dashboard. Your progress is synced instantly with your browser cache so nothing ever drops.

#### 🚀 Complete Client-Side Isolation

Everything runs smoothly right inside the DOM. No bulky server dependencies to handle user state, keeping the initial payload incredibly light while providing a highly interactive local playground.
