> [!TIP]
> Version 0.9.0 officially introduces **Facts** — a premium, highly interactive knowledge base strictly curated for senior engineers.

### What's New?

#### 🧠 Facts: The Knowledge Base
- **First Wave of 100 Facts**: We are launching this feature with exactly 100 meticulously researched technical insights. Dive deep into system design phenomena, database optimizations, tricky microservice orchestrations, and hidden frontend bottlenecks.
- **Interactive Reading UX**: Say goodbye to endless walls of text. Every fact is intelligently broken down into intuitive, clickable tabs (`Concept`, `Why`, `Implementation`, `Pitfalls`) so you can jump straight to the information that matters most.

#### 🌍 Expanding the Homepage
- **Interactive 3D Globe**: We've added an immersive, fully interactive 3D keyword sphere to the homepage. Give it a spin to discover trending engineering concepts and jump directly into the new Facts ecosystem!
- **Studio Aesthetics**: The newly created Facts section perfectly matches our signature premium look. Enjoy the crisp "Glassmorphism & Grid-Dot" workspace backgrounds for distraction-free reading sessions.
