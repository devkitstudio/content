To ensure the new Facts system runs blazingly fast and scales effectively, we significantly upgraded the underlying architecture powering DevKit Studio.

### 1. Lightning-Fast Content Delivery
- **Instant Access**: Instead of relying on traditional databases that slow down page loads, the Facts engine is compiled directly into the application. This means when you click on a fact, the content loads instantaneously with absolutely zero network delay.
- **Improved Discoverability**: Restructured our internal content maps so that every single Fact is efficiently indexed and instantly accessible via direct links perfectly optimized for reading.

### 2. Fluid Visual Performance
- **Optimized 3D Engine**: Integrated a highly optimized rendering process for the new 3D interactive globe on the homepage. By safely isolating the complex graphical workload, the rest of the workspace remains perfectly smooth and responsive on any device.
- **Seamless Navigation**: Engineered a highly responsive interactive viewer specifically for Facts. Switching between different sections (Concept, Why, Implementation, Pitfalls) is a fluid, native experience with absolutely zero stuttering or reloading.
