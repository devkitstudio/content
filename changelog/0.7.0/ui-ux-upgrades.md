> [!TIP]
> The DevKit Studio homepage has been upgraded to provide a more intuitive and visually impressive onboarding experience.

### Landing Page & Navigation Enhancements

#### 🚀 Complete 3D Models Showcase Redesign

- **Asymmetrical Bento Grid:** We've replaced the standard grid layout with a stunning, modern **Bento Box Grid** to visually separate the `3D Models` section from the rest of the page.
- **Visual Impact:** Features a large hero card spanning two columns with an emerald gradient, a focused feature card, and a massive full-width horizontal call-to-action banner at the bottom.

#### 🎯 Smooth Contextual Navigation

- **Scroll-to-Section Routing:** The top navigation bar on the Landing Page now utilizes smooth-scroll anchors instead of hard route navigation. Clicking "Tools", "Algorithms", "Practices", or "3D Models" will elegantly glide you down to the respective section on the homepage, keeping you immersed in the experience before you decide to launch a specific application.
- **Header Border Resolution:** Overhauled the navigation bar's fixed scroll state. The inner pill-shaped menu container now seamlessly merges its bounds with the outer header element when scrolled down. This completely eliminates the restrictive "double frame" and visually distracting duplicate borders.
