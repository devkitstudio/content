### System Stability & Rendering

#### 🐛 "Page Not Found" Formatting Glitch
- We resolved a visual glitch where text could occasionally appear misaligned when landing on the 404 error page.
- This was caused by an internal formatting inconsistency when your browser tried to initially load the screen. We've refined the layout mechanism to ensure it always displays perfectly under any circumstance.

#### 🐛 Schema Generator Stability
- We addressed a rare issue that could prevent the Schema Generator tool from opening successfully.
- The platform's internal icon framework within the Schema Generator has been completely rebuilt. You will now experience identical, beautiful visuals but with faster load times and enhanced stability.
